Heredity and Evolution - Class 10 Biology

1. Introduction to Heredity

Heredity: The transmission of characters from parents to offspring.

Variation: Differences in characters among individuals of a species.

2. Mendel's Experiments

Monohybrid Cross (TT × tt):

        P Generation:   Tall (TT) × Dwarf (tt)
                        ↓
        F1 Generation:  All Tt (Tall)
                        ↓
        F2 Generation:  3 Tall : 1 Dwarf

3. Important Terms

Term Definition
Gene Unit of heredity
Allele Different forms of a gene
Genotype Genetic constitution (e.g., TT, Tt)
Phenotype Physical appearance (e.g., Tall)
Dominant Expressed in F1 generation (T)
Recessive Not expressed in F1 (t)

4. Evolution

Homologous vs Analogous Organs

Homologous: Similar structure, different function (e.g., human arm & bird wing)

Analogous: Different structure, similar function (e.g., bird wing & insect wing)

5. Human Evolution Timeline

Dryopithecus → Ramapithecus → Australopithecus → Homo habilis → Homo erectus → Homo sapiens
