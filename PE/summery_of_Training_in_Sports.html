02 periodization
üìÖ Sports Training Cycles (Periodization)
Training ko teen main cycles mein divide kiya jata hai:
‚Ä¢ Macro Cycle: Yeh sabse bada cycle hai jo pure saal bhar (year-long) chalta hai.

03 strength
üí™ Strength (Taqat)
Muscle dwara resistance ke against lagaya gaya force Strength hai.
‚Ä¢ Static Strength (Isometric): Ismein movement dikhayi nahi deta (e.g., plank, pushing a wall).
‚Ä¢ Dynamic Strength (Isotonic/Isokinetic): Ismein muscle ka movement visible hota hai (e.g., weights uthana ya cycling).
‚Ä¢ Types: maximum strength, explosive strength (jump ke liye), aur strength endurance (lambe waqt tak force lagana).
static ¬∑ dynamic
04 endurance
üèÉ Endurance (Sahan-shakti)
Thakan (fatigue) ke bawajood kisi kaam ko lambe waqt tak karne ki ability.
‚Ä¢ Continuous Training Method: Bina ruke lambi distance tak daudna (Slow, Fast, aur Variable types).
‚Ä¢ Interval Training Method: Ismein workout ke beech-beech mein rest (recovery period) liya jata hai.
‚Ä¢ Fartlek Training: Yeh nature ya jungle mein kiya jata hai jahan terrains badalte rehte hain aur speed bhi change hoti rehti hai.
continuous ¬∑ interval ¬∑ fartlek
05 speed
‚ö° Speed
Fastest possible rate par body movement karne ki ability. Iske types mein reaction time, acceleration, aur locomotor ability (maximum speed ko maximum distance tak maintain karna) aate hain. Isse Acceleration runs aur Pace runs ke zariye improve kiya ja sakta hai.
reaction ¬∑ acceleration
06 flexibility
üßò Flexibility
Joints ki maximum range of movement.
‚Ä¢ Active Flexibility: Bina kisi external help ke (e.g., stretching khud karna).
‚Ä¢ Passive Flexibility: Partner ya kisi equipment ki help se.
‚Ä¢ PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation): Yeh advanced athletes ke liye ek stretching technique hai jismein partner ki help li jati hai.
active ¬∑ passive ¬∑ PNF
07 coordinative
üîÑ Coordinative Abilities
Body movements ko perfection aur efficiency ke saath karne ki ability.
‚Ä¢ Balance Ability: Body ko stable position mein rakhna.
‚Ä¢ Reaction Ability: Kisi stimulus par turant respond karna.
‚Ä¢ Coupling Ability: Body ke alag-alag parts ko ek saath move karke specific movement karna (e.g., Dhoni ka helicopter shot).
balance ¬∑ reaction ¬∑ coupling

üîÑ Circuit Training
