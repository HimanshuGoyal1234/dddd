Chapter 3: Yoga as Preventive Measure for Lifestyle Diseases

1. Introduction to Yoga and Lifestyle Diseases

What are Lifestyle Diseases?

Lifestyle diseases are disorders associated with the way people live and work. These include:

  • Obesity
  • Diabetes (Type 2)
  • Hypertension
  • Cardiovascular diseases
  • Chronic respiratory diseases
  • Stress-related disorders

Yoga as Preventive Therapy

Yoga helps prevent lifestyle diseases through:

Physical Benefits

Improves flexibility, strength and posture

Physiological Benefits

Enhances respiratory, circulatory and digestive functions

Psychological Benefits

Reduces stress, anxiety and depression

Lifestyle Benefits

Promotes healthy habits and self-discipline

2. Yoga for Specific Lifestyle Diseases

Yoga for Obesity

Recommended Asanas: Surya Namaskar, Dhanurasana, Bhujangasana, Vajrasana

Pranayama: Kapalbhati, Bhastrika

Benefits: Boosts metabolism, burns fat, improves digestion

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

12-step sequence that provides complete workout

Yoga for Diabetes

Recommended Asanas: Mandukasana, Paschimottanasana, Ardha Matsyendrasana

Pranayama: Bhramari, Nadi Shodhana

Benefits: Stimulates pancreas, improves insulin sensitivity

Yoga for Hypertension

  • Shavasana (Corpse Pose)
  • Viparita Karani (Legs-up-the-wall)
  • Balasana (Child's Pose)
  • Pranayama: Sheetali, Sheetkari

Yoga for Heart Health

  • Tadasana (Mountain Pose)
  • Utthita Trikonasana (Triangle Pose)
  • Pranayama: Anulom Vilom
  • Meditation techniques

3. Important Yoga Asanas and Their Benefits

Asana Procedure Benefits Contraindications
Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose) Lie prone, place hands near chest, lift upper body Strengthens spine, improves digestion Pregnancy, hernia
Dhanurasana (Bow Pose) Lie prone, hold ankles, lift chest and thighs Improves posture, massages abdominal organs High BP, migraine
Ardha Matsyendrasana (Half Spinal Twist) Sit with legs extended, bend right knee, twist torso Improves spinal flexibility, stimulates pancreas Spinal injury, pregnancy

4. Pranayama Techniques for Stress Management

Key Pranayama Techniques

Kapalbhati

Technique: Forceful exhalations with passive inhalations

Benefits: Cleanses respiratory system, improves digestion

Anulom Vilom

Technique: Alternate nostril breathing

Benefits: Balances nervous system, reduces stress

Bhramari

Technique: Humming bee sound during exhalation

Benefits: Calms mind, relieves tension

Sheetali

Technique: Cooling breath through curled tongue

Benefits: Cools body, reduces anxiety
