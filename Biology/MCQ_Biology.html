Class 12 Biology - Top 15 MCQs per Chapter

Chapter 1: Genetics

1. The physical basis of heredity is:
(a) RNA
(b) DNA
(c) Histones
(d) Chromosomes
Answer: (d) Chromosomes
2. The genotype of a plant showing a recessive trait is:
(a) TT
(b) Tt
(c) tt
(d) TT or Tt
Answer: (c) tt
3. A cross between two tall pea plants resulted in offspring with some dwarf plants. This indicates that:
(a) Both parents are homozygous tall
(b) Both parents are heterozygous tall
(c) One parent is homozygous tall and the other is heterozygous tall
(d) One parent is homozygous tall and the other is dwarf
Answer: (b) Both parents are heterozygous tall
4. In a dihybrid cross, the ratio of phenotypes in the F2 generation is:
(a) 1:1:1:1
(b) 3:1
(c) 9:3:3:1
(d) 1:2:1
Answer: (c) 9:3:3:1
5. The phenomenon of "one gene affecting multiple traits" is called:
(a) Epistasis
(b) Pleiotropy
(c) Polygeny
(d) Codominance
Answer: (b) Pleiotropy
6. The genetic material in bacteria is:
(a) DNA
(b) RNA
(c) DNA and RNA
(d) Protein
Answer: (a) DNA
7. The process of transfer of genetic material from one bacterium to another through a virus is called:
(a) Transformation
(b) Transduction
(c) Conjugation
(d) Translation
Answer: (b) Transduction
8. The genetic code is:
(a) Universal
(b) Ambiguous
(c) Overlapping
(d) Non-degenerate
Answer: (a) Universal
9. The number of chromosomes in a human gamete is:
(a) 23
(b) 46
(c) 22
(d) 44
Answer: (a) 23
10. The condition in which a gene suppresses the effect of another non-allelic gene is called:
(a) Dominance
(b) Epistasis
(c) Pleiotropy
(d) Codominance
Answer: (b) Epistasis
11. The process of formation of mRNA from DNA is called:
(a) Translation
(b) Transcription
(c) Replication
(d) Mutation
Answer: (b) Transcription
12. The number of barr bodies in a female with Turner's syndrome is:
(a) 0
(b) 1
(c) 2
(d) 3
Answer: (a) 0
13. The phenomenon of "both alleles expressing themselves in a heterozygous condition" is called:
(a) Dominance
(b) Codominance
(c) Epistasis
(d) Pleiotropy
Answer: (b) Codominance
14. The genetic disorder caused due to the presence of an extra chromosome number 21 is:
(a) Turner's syndrome
(b) Klinefelter's syndrome
(c) Down's syndrome
(d) Sickle cell anemia
Answer: (c) Down's syndrome
15. The process of change in the genetic material is called:
(a) Mutation
(b) Recombination
(c) Translation
(d) Transcription
Answer: (a) Mutation
